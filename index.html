KYF was established in 2013, and its is a non-profit youth organization independent of all political affiliation and based on philosophy for and with rural Youth.

KYF strives to increase youth participation, support socially disadvantage youth in personal development and acquiring necessary job search skills, develop intellectual abilities and professional skills of youth, involve youth in volunteer work, form civil society through the youth movement, ensure youth participation in local and regional life, seeks for rural tourism promotion, and develop mutual relationships.
